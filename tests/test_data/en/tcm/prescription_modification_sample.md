# Formula Modification Logic Sample

## Guizhi Tang (Cinnamon Twig Decoction) Modifications

### Original Formula
Guizhi Tang: Cinnamon twig three liang (removed bark), peony three liang, licorica two liang (honey-fried), fresh ginger three liang (sliced), jujube twelve pieces (broken).

### Modification 1: Accompanying Neck and Back Stiffness
If the disease is accompanied by neck and back stiffness, **add Pueraria four liang**.

### Modification 2: Accompanying Asthma
If the disease is accompanied by asthma, **add Magnolia bark two liang, apricot kernel fifty pieces**.

### Modification 3: Accompanying Abdominal Fullness and Pain
If the disease is accompanied by abdominal fullness and pain, **remove peony, add Aconite one piece**.

### Modification 4: Accompanying Yang Deficiency with Sweating
If the disease is accompanied by yang deficiency with sweating, **remove peony, add Aconite one piece**.

## Mahuang Tang (Ephedra Decoction) Modifications

### Original Formula
Mahuang Tang: Ephedra three liang (removed nodes), cinnamon twig two liang (removed bark), licorica one liang (honey-fried), apricot kernel seventy pieces (removed skin and tip).

### Modification 1: Accompanying Internal Heat
If the disease is accompanied by internal heat, **remove cinnamon twig, add Gypsum half jin**.

### Modification 2: Accompanying Dampness
If the disease is accompanied by dampness, **add Atractylodes two liang**.

## Xiaochaihu Tang (Minor Bupleurum Decoction) Modifications

### Original Formula
Xiaochaihu Tang: Bupleurum half jin, scutellaria three liang, ginseng three liang, licorica three liang (honey-fried), fresh ginger three liang (sliced), jujube twelve pieces (broken), pinellia half sheng (washed).

### Modification 1: Chest Restlessness without Vomiting
If the disease is accompanied by chest restlessness without vomiting, **remove Pinellia and Ginseng, add Trichosanthes fruit one piece**.

### Modification 2: Accompanying Thirst
If the disease is accompanied by thirst, **remove Pinellia, add Ginseng to total four and a half liang, add Trichosanthes root four liang**.

### Modification 3: Accompanying Abdominal Pain
If the disease is accompanied by abdominal pain, **remove Scutellaria, add Peony three liang**.

### Modification 4: Accompanying Hypochondriac Hardness and Stuffiness
If the disease is accompanied by hypochondriac hardness and stuffiness, **remove Jujube, add Ostrea four liang**.

### Modification 5: Accompanying Palpitations below the Heart
If the disease is accompanied by palpitations below the heart, **remove Scutellaria, add Poria four liang**.

### Modification 6: No Thirst with Mild External Heat
If the disease is accompanied by no thirst with mild external heat, **remove Ginseng, add Cinnamon twig three liang, cover to induce slight sweating and cure**.

### Modification 7: Accompanying Cough
If the disease is accompanied by cough, **remove Ginseng, Jujube, and Fresh ginger, add Schisandra half sheng, dried ginger two liang**.
