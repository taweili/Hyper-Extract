# Materia Medica Properties - Single Herb Sample

## Ginseng (Ren Shen)

### Name Explanation
Ginseng, also known as Huang Shen, Xue Shen, Ren Xian, Gui Gai, Shen Cao, Tu Jing, Di Jing, Hai Yu, Zhou Mian Huan Dan.

### Collected Explanations
"Bie Lu" states: Ginseng grows in the valleys of Shangdang and Liaodong. The roots are harvested in February, April, and early August, scraped with bamboo knife and dried in shade, must not be exposed to wind.

### Properties
Sweet, slightly cold, non-toxic.

### Main Indications
Tonifies the five zang organs, calms the spirit and consciousness, stabilizes the soul and ethereal soul, stops palpitations and fright, eliminates pathogenic factors, improves vision, opens the heart and benefits intelligence. Long-term use lightens the body and extends lifespan.

### Channel Entries
Enters the Spleen meridian, Lung meridian, Heart meridian
