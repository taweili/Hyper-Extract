## 1. Factory Overview

Factory A is a modern manufacturing facility under the company, located within the industrial park, covering an area of approximately 50,000 square meters. The factory primarily engages in electronic product manufacturing and precision machining business.

### 1.1 Factory Scale

| Item | Data |
|------|------|
| Land Area | 50,000 m² |
| Building Area | 35,000 m² |
| Number of Employees | Approximately 500 |
| Annual Output | 500 million yuan |

### 1.2 Plant Layout

Factory A consists of the following main functional areas:

**Production Areas**:
- Manufacturing Workshop A: Automated assembly line (8,000 m²)
- Manufacturing Workshop B: Precision machining center (6,000 m²)
- Manufacturing Workshop C: Assembly workshop (5,000 m²)

**Supporting Areas**:
- Warehouse: Raw material and finished product storage (10,000 m²)
- Power Station: Power, water, and gas supply (2,000 m²)
- Office Area: Administrative offices (3,000 m²)

## 2. Production Systems

### 2.1 Manufacturing Workshop A - Automated Assembly Line

Manufacturing Workshop A is the core production workshop, mainly containing the following systems:

**CNC Machining Systems**:
- CNC Machining Center Alpha (TM-5000X, 2 units): Precision parts machining
- CNC Machining Center Beta (TM-5000X, 2 units): Precision parts machining

**Robot Systems**:
- Industrial Robot Arm R1 (AR-200, 3 units): Workpiece handling and assembly
- Industrial Robot Arm R2 (AR-200, 3 units): Workpiece handling and assembly
- Industrial Robot Arm R3 (AR-150, 2 units): Backup robot arms

**Material Handling Systems**:
- Conveyor Belt System (CT-500, 1 set): Workpiece transportation
- Automated Guided Vehicle AGV (LM-1000, 4 units): Material distribution

**Supporting Systems**:
- Cooling System: Provides cooling for CNC equipment
- Lubrication System: Provides lubrication for mechanical equipment
- Hydraulic System: Provides power for pneumatic equipment
- Dust Removal System: Maintains workshop cleanliness

### 2.2 Manufacturing Workshop B - Precision Machining Center

**Turning Systems**: 4 CNC lathes
**Milling Systems**: 3 CNC milling machines
**Grinding Systems**: 2 precision grinders
**Inspection Systems**: Coordinate measuring machine

### 2.3 Manufacturing Workshop C - Assembly Workshop

**Final Assembly Line**: Product final assembly
**Testing Line**: Product functional testing
**Packaging Line**: Product packaging and storage
