## Chapter 1: General Provisions

### 1.1 Purpose

To standardize employee attendance management, establish a scientific and reasonable performance evaluation system, protect the legitimate rights and interests of the Company and employees, and efficiently complete work while slackingly, this policy is formulated in accordance with the "Galactic Labor Law," "Universal Labor Contract Law," and related interstellar laws and regulations, combined with the Company's actual circumstances.

### 1.2 Scope of Application

This policy applies to all regular employees, probationary employees, and alien interns of Universe's Top Slackers Inc. (hereinafter referred to as "the Company").

### 1.3 Basic Principles

1. Balance between slacking and work principle
2. Principle of combining incentives with constraints
3. Principle of linking performance with slacking hours
4. Continuous slacking continuous improvement principle

## Chapter 2: Attendance Management Regulations

### 2.1 Working Hours

#### 2.1.1 Standard Working Hours

- **Workdays**: Monday to Friday (can apply to work from home slacking on any two days per week)
- **Working Hours**: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM (slacking time), 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM (working time)
- **Lunch Break**: 12:00 PM - 2:00 PM (mandatory slacking time)
- **Weekly Working Hours**: Not exceeding 25 hours (remaining time can be freely arranged for slacking)

#### 2.1.2 Flexible Working Hours

The Company implements a **super flexible working system**, allowing employees to flexibly arrange work and slacking within the following ranges:
- Earliest arrival: 9:00 AM
- Latest arrival: 11:00 AM
- Earliest departure: 4:00 PM
- Latest departure: 6:00 PM

As long as 5 hours of work are completed daily, slacking time is unlimited.

#### 2.1.3 Special Positions

For positions requiring 24-hour duty such as space exploration and alien communication, a **shift system** is implemented. Specific scheduling is arranged by department heads, but for every 4 consecutive working hours, 1 hour of slacking is mandatory.

### 2.2 Clock-in Management

#### 2.2.1 Clock-in Methods

Employees must clock in through the Company's interstellar attendance system:
- **Brainwave Clock-in**: Direct clock-in using brain-computer interface
- **Holographic Projection Clock-in**: Clock-in through holographic image
- **Quantum Entanglement Clock-in**: Clock-in from anywhere in the universe

#### 2.2.2 Clock-in Requirements

- Clock-in required twice daily: arrival and departure
- Arrival clock-in should be completed within 30 minutes after arrival
- Departure clock-in should be completed within 30 minutes before or after leaving
- When unable to clock in due to official business (including travel to other planets), an "Outbound Request" should be submitted in advance through the OA system

#### 2.2.3 Forgetting to Clock In

- Forgetting to clock in is allowed 5 times per month
- When forgetting to clock in, a "Supplementary Clock-in Request" must be submitted through the OA system within 48 hours, effective after department head approval
- For the 6th and subsequent times exceeding 5, each instance is treated as 0.5 days of absence (but slacking time can offset it)
