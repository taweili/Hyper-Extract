**Author**: Space Reporter Starry Walker

---

## Core Announcement

Today, **Galactic Travels**, the world's leading commercial space travel company, held a grand press conference at the **Wenchang Space Launch Center**, officially announcing the successful maiden flight of its next-generation manned spacecraft — **Shenzhou-50**!

Galactic Travels Founder and CEO **Richard Xinghe**激动地表示："Shenzhou-50's successful maiden flight marks a new era in human space travel. Our goal is to make space travel accessible to every ordinary person!"

## Shenzhou-50 Highlights

### 1. Revolutionary Propulsion System

Shenzhou-50 is equipped with Galactic Travels' self-developed **"Galactic Engine" nuclear propulsion system**, a major breakthrough in human aerospace history:
- Earth to Mars travel time reduced from 6 months to **30 days**
- Propulsion efficiency improved by 500%
- Maximum speed can reach 2% of the speed of light

### 2. Comfortable Space Cabin Experience

Shenzhou-50 can accommodate 20 passengers, providing a luxurious and comfortable space travel experience:
- Each cabin equipped with panoramic skylights for viewing magnificent cosmic scenery
- Zero-gravity fitness center and recreational area
- Artificial gravity cabin to reduce space discomfort
- Michelin three-star space restaurant

### 3. Safety Assurance System

Addressing special risks of space travel, Shenzhou-50 provides multiple safety assurances:
- Triple-redundant life support system
- Micro black hole protective shield
- Emergency return capsule, capable of returning to Earth within 24 hours
- ISO Space Safety Certification compliant
