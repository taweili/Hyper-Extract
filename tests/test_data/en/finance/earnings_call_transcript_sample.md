## I. Management Remarks

### Emily Zhao — Board Secretary

Good afternoon, investors and analysts. Welcome to Galaxy Intelligent Technology's Q4 and Full Year 2025 Earnings Conference Call. Before we begin, please note that any forward-looking statements regarding future outlook and performance expectations mentioned during this call involve uncertainties, and actual results may differ from expectations.

Now, I would like to invite Mr. Michael Chen to introduce the Company's full-year business performance.

---

### Michael Chen — Chairman and CEO

Thank you, Emily. Good afternoon, investor friends.

2025 is a milestone year in Galaxy Intelligent's development history. The Company achieved operating revenue of RMB 3.968 billion, a year-over-year increase of 27.8%; net profit attributable to parent company shareholders was RMB 362 million, a year-over-year increase of 130.8%. This achievement was made possible by the efforts of all employees and the general trend of digital transformation in China's manufacturing industry.

I would like to emphasize three key aspects:

**First, breakthrough progress in the key customer strategy.** During the reporting period, we signed multiple industry-leading customers, including CATL, BYD and SMIC. The acquisition of these benchmark customers not only brought direct revenue contribution but, more importantly, validated our product competitiveness in the high-end manufacturing sector. The number of customers with annual revenue exceeding RMB 5 million increased from 85 to 128, a growth of 50.6%.

**Second, outstanding performance in the new business segment.** The energy management and carbon neutrality business achieved revenue of RMB 675 million, a year-over-year increase of 125.8%, making it the fastest-growing segment of the Company. With the advancement of carbon peak and carbon neutrality goals and the restart of the CCER market, we expect this segment to maintain growth of over 40% in 2026.

**Third, continuous deepening of technological innovation.** We have made significant progress in integrating large models with industrial AI. The Galaxy Core 3.0 platform introduced an LLM-driven natural language interaction interface, enabling frontline engineers to directly query equipment status and issue maintenance commands through natural language. This feature has been deployed and verified at 85 customer sites with positive feedback.

Now, let me invite CFO Linda Liu to present the financial data.

### Linda Liu — VP and CFO

Thank you, Mr. Chen. Let me present the key financial data for Q4 and the full year.

#### Q4 Performance

Q4 achieved operating revenue of RMB 1.258 billion, a year-over-year increase of 32.5% and a quarter-over-quarter increase of 18.2%. Q4 is typically our peak season, benefiting from customers' year-end budget execution.

Breaking down by business segment:
- **Intelligent Inspection and Quality Control**: Q4 revenue of RMB 568 million, year-over-year increase of 35.2%, with semiconductor industry customers contributing the main growth

- **Energy Management and Carbon Neutrality**: Q4 revenue of RMB 238 million, year-over-year increase of 145.8%, benefiting from the recognition of multiple large carbon management projects

| Operating Revenue | RMB 3.968B | RMB 3.105B | +27.8% |

Operating cash flow increased by 50.7% year-over-year to RMB 428 million, mainly driven by net profit growth and increase in advance payments. Free cash flow was RMB 385 million, a year-over-year increase of 62.8%.

Thank you, CFO Liu. Let me present the Company's latest technology progress.

**Galaxy Core 3.0 Platform Launch**

In September 2025, we officially launched the Galaxy Core 3.0 platform, the most important product upgrade in the Company's history. Key breakthroughs in version 3.0 include:

1. **LLM Integration**: First integration of LLM capabilities into industrial scenarios, supporting natural language queries and command issuance. Customers can directly obtain analysis results through natural language such as "query the equipment failure rate of Workshop C this month."

2. **Multi-modal Fusion**: Integrated visual, acoustic, vibration and other multi-sensor data to achieve comprehensive perception of equipment status.

3. **Edge Computing Capability**: Launched a lightweight edge deployment solution supporting offline deployment at industrial sites to meet data security requirements.

**Customer Expansion**

After the launch of Galaxy Core 3.0, we received strong market feedback. In Q4 2025, we newly signed 42 customers, with 20 upgrading to version 3.0. Benchmark customers include CATL's battery production lines, BYD's welding workshops, and SMIC's wafer fabs.

**2026 Technology Roadmap**

In 2026, we will continue to increase R&D investment, with key directions including:
- **Galaxy Core 3.5 Version**: Enhance multi-agent collaboration capabilities, supporting automated decision-making in complex industrial scenarios
- **Industrial Foundation Models**: Train vertical models for specific industrial scenarios to further reduce deployment costs
- **Overseas Version Products**: Develop international versions supporting multiple languages to serve overseas customers

## II. Q&A Session

### Q1: Analyst Liu from CICC

Congratulations on the excellent results. My question is about the energy management business. Could you please detail the growth drivers for this business? Also, what is the gross margin level for this business?

### Michael Chen

Thank you, Analyst Liu. The energy management business is a new business segment we established in 2023. In 2025, it achieved excellent growth with revenue of RMB 675 million, a year-over-year increase of 125.8%.

The growth drivers mainly include:

1. **Policy Push**: Under the dual carbon goals, more and more enterprises have demands for carbon emission management. Especially with the expected restart of the CCER market in 2025, it brings a large amount of incremental demand.

2. **Improved Product Maturity**: After more than two years of development, our products have become relatively mature and can meet customers' actual needs.

3. **Benchmark Customer Effect**: We signed multiple industry-leading customers, and the demonstration effect of these customers drove follow-up purchases from small and medium customers.

Regarding gross margin, the current gross margin of the energy management business is approximately 38%, lower than the Company's overall gross margin of 44.2%. The main reason is that this business requires more on-site implementation work. However, as the proportion of subscription revenue increases, we expect this business's gross margin to gradually improve.

---

### Q2: Analyst Wang from Huatai Securities

I have two questions. First, regarding the increase in subscription revenue proportion, the Company mentioned it reached 65% in 2025 and the target is 80% in 2027. What is the path for this? Second, accounts receivable has also grown relatively quickly. Could you explain the reasons and collection plans?

### Linda Liu

Thank you, Analyst Wang. Let me answer these two questions.

Regarding subscription revenue proportion:

In 2025, we reached 65% subscription revenue proportion, an increase of 13 percentage points from 52% in 2024. The improvement path mainly includes:

1. **New Contracts**: In 2025 new contracts, the subscription model accounted for 75%
2. **Existing Customer Conversion**: Some original project-based customers were converted to subscription model
3. **Upsell Sales**: Upsells to existing customers basically adopt the subscription model

We are very confident in achieving the 80% target by 2027. With the promotion of Galaxy Core 3.0, we expect the subscription proportion of new contracts to further increase.

Regarding accounts receivable:

Indeed, the accounts receivable balance at the end of 2025 was RMB 684 million, a significant increase from RMB 489 million at the end of 2024. The main reasons are:
1. Revenue scale increased by 27.8%, and accounts receivable grew synchronously
2. Q4 revenue proportion increased, and Q4 collections typically come in the following year

Regarding collection, we have taken the following measures:
1. Strengthened accounts receivable management, establishing a tiered early warning mechanism
2. Implemented special follow-up for key customers
3. Optimized contract terms to increase advance payment proportion

We expect accounts receivable turnover days to gradually improve in 2026.

### Q3: Institutional Investor

My question is about the competitive landscape. How does the Company view competition with leaders like Hikvision? What are the Company's differentiated technology advantages?

### David Wang

Thank you for this investor's question.

Regarding competition, we have always believed that healthy competition benefits the overall development of the industry. The industrial AI market is very large, and different companies have different positioning and advantages.

Our differentiated advantages mainly include:

1. **Vertical Depth**: We focus on industrial scenarios and have a deeper understanding of manufacturing. Our products are not general AI, but professional AI that truly solves industrial problems.

2. **Platform Capability**: The Galaxy platform supports multiple industrial application scenarios, allowing customers to solve various problems on one platform. This is more attractive than single-point solutions.

3. **Data Accumulation**: We have served over 1,280 customers and accumulated a large amount of industrial data, which helps our models continuously optimize.

4. **Customer Stickiness**: Our 92% customer renewal rate and 125% revenue renewal rate demonstrate customers' recognition of our products.

Of course, we will continue to monitor competitors' movements and continuously improve our competitiveness.
