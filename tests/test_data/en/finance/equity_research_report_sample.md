## Investment Thesis

We maintain our "Buy" rating on Galaxy Intelligent and raise the target price to RMB 85.60 (from RMB 68.00), based on three core investment logics:

1. **Sustained Industrial AI Momentum**: Driven by the "14th Five-Year Plan" Intelligent Manufacturing Development Plan and manufacturing digital transformation policies, China's industrial AI market reached RMB 48.5 billion in 2025, with 2025-2028 CAGR expected at 25%. As the leading industrial AI software company, the Company's market share increased from 6.8% to 8.2%, continuously consolidating its industry leadership position.

2. **Accelerating Subscription Transformation Unlocking Profitability**: The Company's software subscription revenue proportion increased from 52% in 2024 to 65%, with a target of 80% by 2027. Subscription model gross margin is approximately 82% (vs. project-based 55%), driving comprehensive gross margin from 41.2% to 44.2%. We expect net margin to further improve from 9.1% to 12%-15% in 2026-2027.

3. **Carbon Neutrality Business Opens Second Growth Curve**: The energy management and carbon neutrality business achieved 125.8% revenue growth in 2025. Driven by dual carbon policies and EU CBAM, it is expected to maintain growth above 40% in 2026. This business currently accounts for only 17% of Company revenue, with enormous penetration potential.

## Catalysts Analysis

### Near-term Catalysts (0-6 months)

- **2025 Annual Report Disclosure** (expected April 2026): We expect full-year revenue of RMB 3.97 billion, exceeding market consensus of RMB 3.85 billion. Net profit of RMB 362 million, exceeding consensus of RMB 320 million. Earnings beat could drive valuation re-rating.
- **Galaxy Core 3.0 Mass Rollout**: Expected to add 200-300 customer upgrades to version 3.0 in H1 2026. LLM integration features will enhance product differentiation.
- **CCER Market Restart**: National CCER (China Certified Emission Reduction) market expected to officially restart in Q1 2026, which will directly drive demand for Company carbon management software.

### Mid-term Catalysts (6-18 months)

- **Semiconductor Industry Recovery**: Global semiconductor inventory adjustment expected to complete in H1 2026. The Company's acquired CloudInspect Technology will fully benefit from wafer fab capacity utilization recovery. Semiconductor-related revenue expected to reach RMB 350-450 million in 2026.
- **Overseas Market Expansion**: Company plans to establish regional headquarters in Singapore in 2026, targeting overseas revenue proportion of 10% by 2027. Cooperation with FANUC is expected to materialize in 2026.

### Long-term Catalysts (18+ months)

- **Industrial Foundation Models**: Company is developing vertical foundation models for industrial scenarios, expected to launch in 2027-2028, which could significantly reduce customization costs for AI deployment and expand addressable market.
- **M&A Integration**: Company has RMB 850 million in cash on hand with low leverage, enabling continued acquisition integration in industrial AI segments.

## Risk Analysis

### Downside Risks

1. **Macroeconomic Downturn Risk** (Probability: Medium, Timeframe: 6-12 months): If manufacturing PMI continues below 50, customers may delay IT investments. We estimate that for every 1 percentage point decline in GDP, Company revenue growth could slow by 3-5 percentage points.

2. **Intensifying Competition Risk** (Probability: Medium, Timeframe: 12-24 months): Competitors such as Siemens MindSphere and Huawei FusionPlant are accelerating promotions. Hikvision may pose competitive pressure on the Company's intelligent inspection business leveraging its advantages in visual technology. If Company's market share declines by 1 percentage point, corresponding revenue impact is approximately RMB 40 million.

3. **Accounts Receivable Risk** (Probability: Low, Timeframe: Ongoing): Company accounts receivable balance is RMB 684 million, accounting for 17.2% of revenue. If some key customers face payment difficulties, it may generate bad debt losses.

4. **Talent Loss Risk** (Probability: Low-Medium, Timeframe: Ongoing): AI industry talent competition is intense. Loss of core technical personnel may affect product R&D progress. Currently core team attrition rate is 8.5%, below industry average of 12%, but requires continuous attention.

5. **Goodwill Impairment Risk** (Probability: Low, Timeframe: 12-24 months): Acquisition of CloudInspect generated goodwill of RMB 125 million. If CloudInspect business falls short of expectations, there is goodwill impairment risk.
