## Section 2: Company Introduction and Key Financial Indicators

### 2.1 Company Introduction

Galaxy Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise specializing in industrial artificial intelligence and intelligent manufacturing solutions. The Company primarily provides AI-based intelligent inspection, predictive maintenance and production optimization software platforms for manufacturing, energy, transportation and other industries. The Company's self-developed "Galaxy Core" AI platform has served over 1,280 enterprise customers, covering 28 provinces and cities nationwide and 8 overseas countries and regions.

### 2.2 Main Business Segments

The Company's business is divided into three major segments:

**1. Intelligent Inspection and Quality Control (45% of revenue)**

The Company's flagship product "Galaxy Vision" utilizes deep learning and computer vision technology to provide Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) solutions for semiconductor, new energy vehicle, consumer electronics and other industries. During the reporting period, the intelligent inspection business achieved operating revenue of RMB 1.785 billion, a year-over-year increase of 28.5%.

**2. Predictive Maintenance (38% of revenue)**

The "Galaxy Predict" product achieves equipment fault prediction and maintenance optimization through industrial IoT sensor data collection and AI analysis. During the reporting period, it achieved operating revenue of RMB 1.508 billion, a year-over-year increase of 35.2%.

**3. Energy Management and Carbon Neutrality (17% of revenue)**

The energy management business segment established in 2023 provides factory energy consumption optimization and carbon emission management solutions. During the reporting period, it achieved operating revenue of RMB 675 million, a year-over-year increase of 125.8%.

## Section 3: Management Discussion and Analysis

### 3.1 Discussion and Analysis of Business Operations During the Reporting Period

In 2025, the Company achieved operating revenue of RMB 3.968 billion, a year-over-year increase of 27.8%; net profit attributable to shareholders of the listed company was RMB 362 million, a year-over-year increase of 130.8%. The significant growth in business performance was mainly driven by the following factors:

**1. Strong Industry Demand**

Driven by the national "14th Five-Year Plan" Intelligent Manufacturing Development Plan and manufacturing digital transformation policies, the industrial AI software market maintained high-speed growth. According to data from CCID Research Institute, China's industrial AI market reached RMB 48.5 billion in 2025, a year-over-year increase of 32.5%. Leveraging its technology leadership and brand accumulation, the Company's market share increased from 6.8% in 2024 to 8.2%.

**2. Significant Results from Key Customer Strategy**

During the reporting period, the number of customers with annual revenue exceeding RMB 5 million increased from 85 in 2024 to 128, a year-over-year increase of 50.6%. The combined revenue from the top 10 customers accounted for 38.5%, a decrease from 42.3% in the previous year, indicating continuous optimization of customer concentration. New signings of industry leaders such as CATL, BYD and SMIC further validated the Company's product competitiveness in high-end manufacturing.

Among newly signed orders, the average contract value increased from RMB 6.8 million in 2024 to RMB 9.2 million, a growth of 35.3%. Customer renewal rate reached 92%, and revenue renewal rate (including upsells) reached 125%, demonstrating strong product stickiness and continuous cross-selling capability.

**3. Gross Margin Improvement**

The Company's comprehensive gross margin increased from 41.2% in 2024 to 44.2%, mainly due to: (1) the increase in software subscription revenue proportion from 52% to 65%, with subscription model gross margin of approximately 82%, higher than the 55% of project-based implementation model; (2) improved AI model inference efficiency reduced cloud computing resource consumption, with unit computing costs decreasing by approximately 25%; (3) economies of scale improved the personnel efficiency of the customer success team.

**4. Continuous Increase in R&D Investment**

During the reporting period, R&D investment was RMB 714 million, a year-over-year increase of 27.8%, accounting for 18% of operating revenue. The total number of R&D personnel reached 685, accounting for 42% of total employees. Main R&D directions include:
- Galaxy Core 3.0 Platform Upgrade: Introduction of LLM-driven natural language interaction interface
- Cross-modal AI Fusion: Integration of visual, acoustic, vibration and other multi-source data
- Edge Computing Solutions: Edge deployment solutions for offline and low-latency scenarios
- Carbon Footprint Tracking: Carbon emission accounting engine compliant with ISO 14064 and GHG Protocol

### 3.2 Key Business Risks During the Reporting Period

**1. Technology Iteration Risk**

Artificial intelligence technology is evolving rapidly, and the rapid advancement of Large Language Models (LLM) and generative AI may change the technological paradigm of industrial AI applications. If the Company fails to timely track technology development trends and complete product iteration and upgrades, it may face the risk of technological backwardness. The Company will continue to increase R&D investment, planning to maintain R&D expense ratio at 18%-20% in 2026.

**2. Intensifying Market Competition Risk**

The industrial AI market has attracted many competitors, including international giants such as Siemens and Schneider Electric, as well as domestic leading enterprises such as Hikvision and iFlytek. Some competitors have advantages in capital, channels and brand. Intensifying competition may lead to product price pressure and increased customer acquisition costs.

**3. Accounts Receivable Risk**

As the business scale expands, the Company's accounts receivable balance continues to grow. As of December 31, 2025, accounts receivable balance was RMB 684 million, accounting for 17.2% of operating revenue. Although the aging is mainly concentrated within 1 year (accounting for 82%), large accounts receivable still carry collection risk. The Company has accrued bad debt provisions of RMB 34.2 million, with bad debt provision accrual ratio of 5%.

**4. Talent Loss Risk**

The Company's core technical team is key to its competitiveness. During the reporting period, the core technical personnel loss rate was 8.5%, lower than the industry average of 12%. The Company has implemented an equity incentive plan, granting 8.5 million stock options to 168 core employees to enhance talent retention.

## Section 5: Significant Events

### 5.1 Major Acquisitions

In August 2025, the Company acquired 100% equity of Shanghai CloudInspect Technology Co., Ltd. for cash consideration of RMB 225 million. CloudInspect is a leading domestic semiconductor wafer inspection AI software provider with over 28 semiconductor industry customers. After the acquisition, the Company's market share in the semiconductor inspection field increased from 5.2% to 8.5%.

The acquisition generated goodwill of RMB 125 million. The Company has conducted impairment tests on goodwill in accordance with Accounting Standards for Business Enterprises, and no impairment occurred in 2025.

### 5.2 Litigation and Arbitration

During the reporting period, the Company had no significant litigation or arbitration matters. The patent infringement dispute with Shenzhen SmartInspect Technology Co., Ltd. disclosed in 2024 was settled in September 2024, and the Company did not bear any compensation liability.
