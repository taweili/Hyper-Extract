## Section 1: Company Overview

### 1.1 Company Introduction

QuantumCloud Technology Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "QuantumCloud," "the Company" or "the Issuer") was established in 2018 as a high-tech enterprise specializing in quantum computing simulation and quantum security encryption technology. The Company's headquarters is located in Hefei High-tech Industrial Development Zone, with R&D centers in Beijing, Shanghai and Shenzhen.

The Company's main business includes:
1. **Quantum Computing Simulation Platform** (45% of revenue): Quantum circuit simulation software based on classical computers, helping research institutions and enterprises develop and verify quantum algorithms before quantum hardware matures
2. **Quantum Security Encryption Products** (38% of revenue): Post-quantum cryptography (PQC) solutions providing data security protection against quantum computing attacks for finance, government, military and other fields
3. **Quantum Computing Education and Consulting** (17% of revenue): Quantum computing training courses and technical consulting services for universities and enterprises

### 1.2 Company Development History

#### 2018 — Founding

- **March 2018**: Company founded by Co-founders Dr. Raymond Liu (CEO) and Dr. Grace Chen (CTO) in Hefei, based on their research at the Quantum Information Laboratory of University of Science and Technology of China. Dr. Liu previously worked on quantum computing research at D-Wave Systems in Canada
- **May 2018**: Received angel round financing of RMB 5 million, led by Hefei Science and Technology Innovation Investment Co., Ltd.
- **September 2018**: Core team expanded to 15 people; completed quantum circuit simulation engine v0.1 prototype development

#### 2019 — Technology Accumulation

- **April 2019**: Established first quantum computing laboratory in Hefei High-tech Zone, covering 800 square meters
- **August 2019**: Received Series A financing of RMB 30 million, led by CAS Innovation Ventures, with Hefei Industry Investment following
- **December 2019**: Released quantum circuit simulation platform "CloudQ Q-Sim" v1.0, supporting 20-qubit simulation, with leading performance among domestic similar products

#### 2020 — Product Commercialization

- **June 2020**: First commercial customer acquired, signed quantum security encryption POC contract with Bank of China Information Technology Department for RMB 2.8 million
- **November 2020**: Received Series B financing of RMB 120 million, led by Sequoia Capital China, with IDG Capital and Shenzhen Capital following, post-money valuation of RMB 800 million
- **December 2020**: Employee count increased to 52; annual operating revenue of RMB 18.5 million

#### 2021 — Scale Expansion

- **March 2021**: Established R&D center in Beijing Zhongguancun, focusing on productization of quantum security encryption technology
- **July 2021**: Obtained commercial cryptography product model certificate issued by the State Cryptography Administration, becoming one of the first domestic enterprises to obtain post-quantum cryptography product certification
- **October 2021**: Received Series B+ financing of RMB 250 million, led by SDIC Venture, valuation of RMB 1.8 billion
- **December 2021**: Number of contracted customers reached 85; annual operating revenue of RMB 56.8 million

#### 2022 — Business Breakthrough

- **May 2022**: Signed quantum secure communication upgrade project contract with State Grid Information and Communication Industry Group for RMB 85 million, the largest single order in the Company's history
- **September 2022**: "CloudQ Q-Sim" v3.0 released, supporting 50-qubit full-amplitude simulation, partnering with Alibaba Cloud to provide cloud-based quantum simulation services
- **November 2022**: Received Series C financing of RMB 380 million, led by Hillhouse Ventures, valuation of RMB 2.8 billion
- **December 2022**: Employee count increased to 185; annual operating revenue of RMB 235 million; net loss of RMB 18.5 million

#### 2023 — Industry Deepening

- **April 2023**: Established Financial Industry Solutions Center in Shanghai Zhangjiang, focusing on providing quantum security solutions for banking, securities and insurance industries
- **August 2023**: Signed strategic cooperation agreements with ICBC, CCB and China Merchants Bank to promote quantum security encryption upgrades in the financial industry
- **October 2023**: Quantum security VPN product obtained CCRC information security certification
- **December 2023**: Number of contracted customers reached 280; annual operating revenue of RMB 452 million; net loss of RMB 32.5 million

#### 2024 — Technology Milestone

- **March 2024**: "CloudQ Q-Sim" v5.0 released, supporting 100-qubit simulation and quantum error correction simulation functions, ranking among the top three globally in international quantum computing simulation benchmarks
- **August 2024**: Received Series D financing of RMB 600 million, led by National Manufacturing Transformation and Upgrade Fund, valuation of RMB 5.2 billion
- **December 2024**: Employee count increased to 420; annual operating revenue of RMB 680 million; net loss of RMB 18.5 million

#### 2025 — Approaching Profitability

- **March 2025**: Signed quantum security government cloud project contract with Shenzhen Municipal Government Data Administration Bureau for RMB 120 million
- **June 2025**: Started overseas business, signed quantum security cooperation MOUs with Singapore Telecom and Korea's KT Corporation
- **October 2025**: Number of contracted customers reached 520; annual operating revenue of RMB 920 million; net loss narrowed to RMB 4.2 million

#### Q3 2025 — First Profitability and IPO Preparation

- **September 2025**: Achieved profitability for the first time in a single quarter (Q3 2025 net profit of RMB 18.5 million)
- **November 2025**: Completed joint-stock reform, converted to a joint-stock limited company
- **December 2025**: Submitted IPO application to Shanghai Stock Exchange
