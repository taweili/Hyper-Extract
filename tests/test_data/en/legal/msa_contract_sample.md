## Article 1: Definitions and Interpretations

1.1 **"Project"**: Refers to the XX Management System development project jointly implemented by both parties.

1.2 **"Software Product"**: Refers to the XX Management System v2.0 developed by Party B for Party A as agreed in this Agreement, including front-end interfaces, back-end services, database, and mobile applications.

1.3 **"Technical Documentation"**: Refers to design documents, user manuals, installation guides, operation instructions, and API interface documentation related to the software product.

1.4 **"Intellectual Property"**: Refers to patent rights, copyrights, trademark rights, trade secrets, and other rights related to intellectual achievements.

1.5 **"Confidential Information"**: Refers to trade secrets, technical information, customer data, and business plans disclosed by one party to the other during cooperation that are not known to the public.

1.6 **"Force Majeure"**: Refers to unforeseeable, unavoidable, and insurmountable objective circumstances, including but not limited to natural disasters, war, strikes, government actions, and cyber attacks.

1.7 **"Acceptance Criteria"**: Refers to the functional, performance, and quality requirements of the software product as agreed by both parties, detailed in Appendix "Requirements Specification."

1.8 **"Service Fee"**: Refers to the project development and technical service fees paid by Party A to Party B as agreed in this Agreement, with a total amount of RMB XXX million.

## Article 2: Scope and Content of Services

2.1 **System Development**: Party B shall develop XX Management System v2.0, including the following modules:
   - Production Planning and Scheduling Module
   - Equipment Management Module
   - Quality Traceability Module
   - Data Analysis Dashboard Module

2.2 **Technical Training**: Party B shall provide Party A with no less than 40 person-hours of technical training after software delivery.

2.3 **Operation and Maintenance Services**: During the quality assurance period (12 months from the date of acceptance), Party B shall provide free operation and maintenance services, including:
   - Defect fixes
   - Security patch updates
   - Performance optimization

2.4 **Secondary Development**: During the operation and maintenance period, Party B shall cooperate with Party A for secondary development work not exceeding 200 person-hours.
